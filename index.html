FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

The majority of our challenges are free, yes. We do have some that are premium and require a Pro subscription to access. It will say on each challenge whether they are free or premium, so it's easy to tell the difference.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can absolutely use projects you build on Frontend Mentor in your portfolio; the platform actively encourages developers to showcase their completed challenges as part of their portfolio to demonstrate their front-end development skills.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

If you're stuck on a Frontend Mentor challenge, the best way to get help is to join the Frontend Mentor Discord server and ask questions in the dedicated "help" channel
